## üéØ Content Strategy Implications

### Phase 1 Content Requirements
- **High Volume**: Multiple posts per day across platforms
- **Platform-Specific**: Tailored to each platform's algorithm and audience
- **Value-Driven**: Educational, entertaining, or inspiring content
- **Trend-Aware**: Leveraging current trends and topics

### Phase 2 Content Requirements
- **Personal Touch**: More intimate, direct communication style
- **Exclusive Value**: Content not available on public platforms
- **Regular Cadence**: Consistent delivery schedule
- **Two-Way Communication**: Encouraging responses and interaction

### Phase 3 Content Requirements
- **Premium Quality**: High-value, in-depth content
- **Community-Driven**: Encouraging member interaction
- **Exclusive Access**: Early access to new content/products
- **Networking Opportunities**: Facilitating member connections

## üöÄ Implementation Strategy
